About CoCurri

CoCurri helps schools manage cocurricular activities and enhance the student experience outside the class environment.

CoCurri provides the tools needed for administrators, teachers, students, and parents to collaborate, interact, organize, track and measure students’ involvement in one central place. It’s secure, easy to learn, and it engages everyone involved!
