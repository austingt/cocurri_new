Simplify life after the bell!

CoCurri helps schools manage cocurricular activities and enhance the student experience outside the class environment. CoCurri provides the tools needed for administrators, teachers, students, and parents to collaborate, interact, organize, track and measure students’ involvement in one central place. It’s secure, easy to learn, and it engages everyone involved!

School Administrators

Make it count!

Increase student participation in co-curricular activities and improve academic achievement. CoCurri provides tools to oversee and manage all clubs, events, and student organizations from a school or district. The application tracks all activities and integrate with the school information system to provide a holistic view of students‘ performance.

Teachers

Empowered to grow and cultivate clubs and activities!

Most teachers have little time left in their day to manage student activities. CoCurri allows teachers to set up clubs, sign up students, assign roles, manage the roster, send calendar invitations, email, text, and track student service hours all within a safe and easy platform available anytime, anywhere.

Parents

Stay connected and in the know!

Involving children in co-curricular activities is a great way for them to explore a number of areas beyond academics. But, keeping track of all their activities, fees, forms and locations can be challenging. Cocurri makes it simple for parents to manage all activities from one central platform so nothing is missed.
